HS code primer

Classify correctly. Or pay the price.

In 2024, Ford was billed $365M for misclassifying vans. A viral beach tote brand bumped duties 30% by misclassifying a zinc keychain as stainless steel. Misclassification means delays, penalties, and retroactive duties — and you are the importer of record, not your supplier.

National digits

First 6 digits are globally harmonised. Beyond that varies by country — US uses 10 digits (HTS), EU/UK 8–10, China 13 digits.

BIGGEST MISTAKES
Copying supplier's code — you are legally responsible, not them
Using marketing names ("smart device") instead of material + function
Ignoring country-specific digits beyond first 6
Not updating when product materials change batch-to-batch
Classifying smartwatch as "watch" (should be telecom device 8517.62)
